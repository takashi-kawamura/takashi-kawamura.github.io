About Me

I am a PhD student specializing in climate dynamics and meteorology, with a focus on Northern Hemisphere atmospheric variability, including the Northern Annular Mode (NAM), North Atlantic Oscillation (NAO), and their modulation by ENSO under global warming.

Research Interests

  • Northern Annular Mode (NAM) and North Atlantic Oscillation (NAO)
  • Aleutian–Icelandic Low seesaw (AIS)
  • ENSO teleconnections (EP-ENSO vs CP-ENSO)
  • Large-ensemble AGCM/CGCM experiments (d4PDF, MIROC6, CESM2, MPI-ESM)

  • (In prep.) ENSO modulation of the Aleutian–Icelandic Low seesaw under climate change.
